DIPONEGORO BIG DATA SCHOOL (DBDS)

Diponegoro Big Data School is a two-days webinar series on 25th to 26th November, 2021. This event is organized by Department of Informatics, Universitas Diponegoro

Register Here

OVERVIEW

Department of Informatics, Faculty of Science and Mathematics, Universitas Diponegoro, Semarang, Indonesia held a webinar entitled of Diponegoro Big Data School (DBDS). DBDS is a platform for disclosing research findings, experiences and critical views regarding the advancement of science and technology, especially Cyber Physical System (CPS).

This webinar is the first of the series and expected to be held annually. Topics include CPS in multiple contexts: Big Data, Data Visualization, Machine Learning, Audio Recognition, Computer Vision, and IoT. This event will be held online on November 25th - 26th, 2021, as part of ICICoS 2021. Participants can take part in this event by filling out this registration form.
